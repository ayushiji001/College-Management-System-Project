About the System

This system allows the management and distribution of student exam results efficiently. Faculty can upload results, and students can view their scores anytime.
A Result Management System (RMS) is a software solution designed to help educational institutions like schools, colleges, and universities manage and store results or academic records for students. This system streamlines the process of result computation, record-keeping, and reporting, enabling efficient management and easy retrieval of academic data.
